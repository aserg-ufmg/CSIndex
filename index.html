CSIndex (previously known as CoreBR) provides meaningful and transparent data about Brazilian scientific production in Computer Science. Currently, the project focus on full research papers published in selected conferences. The papers are retrieved from DBLP; see our FAQ for details or open an issue.

New: Search papers by a given author; see the "Search" option in the main menu.
