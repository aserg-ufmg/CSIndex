CSIndexbr provides transparent data about Brazilian scientific production in Computer Science. We index full research papers published by Brazilian professors in selected conferences and journals, since 2014. The papers are retrieved from DBLP; see our FAQ for details. To report missing papers or other questions, use this form. You can also follow us on Twitter or Medium.

See also: Talk about our project at CIn/UFPE * Tip: click on the column headers of tables to sort.
